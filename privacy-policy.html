Privacy Policy

A short, plain-language explanation of what we do (and don't do) with your data.

Last updated: July 14, 2026

Overview

Skill-Lite is a static site: tutorials, cheat sheets, and browser-based developer tools. There are no user accounts, no logins, and no server-side database. This policy explains the little bit of data that does touch your device.

Information we collect

We do not run analytics, advertising, or tracking scripts, and we do not collect or store personal information on any server. The only data that exists is:

  • Theme preference — your light/dark/color theme choice is saved in your browser's localStorage so it persists between visits. It never leaves your device.
  • DevTools input — anything you paste into a DevTools utility (SQL Formatter, JSON Formatter, JWT Decoder, etc.) is processed entirely client-side, in your browser. It is never transmitted to a server or stored anywhere.

Newsletter & contact

The "Subscribe" form and the Contact page open your own email client with a pre-filled message addressed to us; the form itself does not submit anything to a server. If you choose to send that email, we receive whatever you include in it (typically your email address and message), which we use solely to respond to you.

Cookies

Skill-Lite does not set cookies.

Third-party links

Tutorial pages may link to external documentation or tools (for example, official Kubernetes or Docker docs). Those sites have their own privacy practices, which we don't control.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, we'll update the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the site after a change means you accept the revised policy.
